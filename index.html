🧮 What is BMI?

Body Mass Index is a simple calculation using your height and weight. It gives a quick indication of whether your weight is healthy for your height.

⚠️ Limitations

BMI doesn't measure body fat directly. Athletes may have a high BMI despite low body fat. Always consult a healthcare professional for a full assessment.

📏 The formula

BMI = weight (kg) ÷ height² (m). For example: 70 kg ÷ (1.75 × 1.75) = 22.9. Simple and universal.
